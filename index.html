Resume Tips That Work

01

Lead with Impact

Use your summary to immediately communicate your value. Mention your years of experience, key specialty, and biggest achievement in 2–3 sentences.

02

Quantify Achievements

Replace vague duties with measurable results. "Increased sales by 30%" is more powerful than "Responsible for sales."

03

Tailor for Each Role

Mirror the keywords from the job description in your resume. ATS systems scan for exact matches before a human ever reads your file.

04

Keep It Clean

Stick to one page for under 10 years experience. Use consistent formatting, clear section headers, and plenty of white space.
